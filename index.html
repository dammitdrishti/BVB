Our Courses

Post-Graduate Diploma Programs designed for success

Industrial Relations and Personnel Management

Human Resources Management

Career Scope

HR-related jobs in private and government sectors, consulting, entrepreneurship.

Course Structure

  • Paper 1: Organizational Behaviour
  • Paper 2: Personnel Management
  • Paper 3: Human Resources Development
  • Paper 4: Industrial Relations
  • Paper 5: Labour Legislation (including Social Security and Social Welfare)
  • Paper 6: Cultural Heritage of India

For Whom?

Fresh Graduates: Those who want to learn Human Resource Development skills without pursuing lengthy and expensive BBA/MBA courses.

Working Professionals: It's a flexible course catering to working professionals who want to learn new skills and grow professionally.

Contact Hours 150 hours
Batch Timings Evening batches

Digital Media

Post-Graduate Diploma Course in Digital Media (Journalism, Social Media and Content Marketing)

Career Scope

News portals, TV News channel, Newspapers, Content Writing and Content Marketing for news and non-news sectors.

Course Structure

  • Paper 1: Understanding Digital Media and related laws
  • Paper 2: Reporting and Content Generation
  • Paper 3: Advanced Journalistic Writing
  • Paper 4: Social Media, Content Marketing & Distribution
  • Paper 5: Multimedia Journalism
  • Paper 6: Cultural Heritage of India

For Whom?

Fresh Graduates: Those who want to make a career in digital media, both news and non-news media without pursuing full-time Journalism and Mass Communication courses.

Working Professionals: It's a flexible course catering to working professionals in print or TV news to switch to digital media. Executives in non-news sector with respect to content generation, marketing and distribution in the digital sector.

Contact Hours 150 hours
Batch Timings Evening classes

International Trade

Post-Graduate Diploma Course in International Trade (Import-Export)

Career Scope

Offices of C&F Agents, CHAs, ICDs, Shipping Lines, ports, airports, railways, container lines, etc. Offices where the execution/documentation of export-import, foreign exchange, etc. is required.

Course Structure

  • Paper 1: Port Operations (Maritime)
  • Paper 2: Supply Chain Management
  • Paper 3: Financial Management for Import-Export Enterprises
  • Paper 4: Global Commercial Ecosystem
  • Paper 5: Export Import Practices, Procedures & Documentation
  • Paper 6: Cultural Heritage of India

For Whom?

Fresh Graduates: It is best suited for those who want to focus only on export-import without having to do an expensive BBA/MBA courses.

Working Professionals:

  • Industrialists and/or entrepreneurs who want to manage/establish international business
  • Executives with experience in Export-Import / International Marketing / Foreign Exchange / Shipping and Logistics
  • Executives from C&F Agents, CHAs, ICDs, Shipping Lines, ports, airports, railways, container lines
  • Officers/Executives from Ministry of Commerce, DGFT, EXIM Bank, Department of Customs, banks
  • Women executives who aspire to re-join the corporate sector after a family break
  • Others who are desirous of learning how international trade is conducted
Contact Hours 150 hours
Batch Timings Evening classes
